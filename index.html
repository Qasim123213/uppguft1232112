Bad Boys Ride or Die

"Bad Boys for Life" (often associated with the phrase "Ride or Die") is the third installment in the "Bad Boys" film series, starring Will Smith as Mike Lowrey and Martin Lawrence as Marcus Burnett. In this action-comedy film, Mike and Marcus, two Miami detectives, team up once again to take down a ruthless assassin linked to Mike's past. As they face deadly threats and personal challenges, they rely on their strong bond and a new elite team to bring justice. The film blends humor, high-octane action, and a deep dive into friendship, loyalty, and redemption.
